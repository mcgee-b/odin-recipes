Deep Dish Pizza

Description

Deep dish pizza is a thick, pan-baked style built with a tall, buttery crust that holds substantial layers. Cheese goes in first, followed by a generous blanket of chunky tomato sauce, creating a dense, structured slice that’s eaten more like a hearty meal than a quick bite.

Ingredients

Crust

  • 3¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons salt
  • 2¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1¼ cups warm water
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • ⅓ cup melted butter

Filling

  • 3–4 cups shredded low-moisture mozzarella
  • ½–1 pound Italian sausage
  • Any additional toppings you want

Sauce

  • 1–2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A pinch of sugar if the tomatoes are too acidic
  • Grated parmesan for finishing
